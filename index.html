Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Still deciding? Here's what most clients ask before their first project.

Short-form turnaround is usually 2–4 days per video. Long-form edits run 5–10 days depending on footage length and complexity. Rush delivery can be arranged on request.

Raw footage via Drive or Frame.io, plus any brand assets — logo, fonts, brand colors — and reference links for the style you're after. The more context up front, the fewer revision rounds later.

Two rounds of revisions are included in every package. Additional rounds are available at a small added cost — most projects wrap well within the included two.

Final files are delivered as MP4 (H.264) by default, in whichever aspect ratios you need — 16:9, 9:16, 1:1 — ready to publish across YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok.

Editing is the core service, but light script structuring and pacing notes are included for SaaS/commercial projects. Full scriptwriting can be added as a separate service — just mention it in the brief.

A 50% deposit secures the project start, with the remaining balance due on final delivery. Ongoing/retainer clients are billed monthly instead.
